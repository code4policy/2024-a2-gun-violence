This site was developed for DPI-691: Programming & Data for Policymakers at the Harvard Kennedy School. In addition to building for our specific users (politician staffers and informed citizens), we considered ourselves users and hoped to practice skills learned through the course. Learning goals for the development team included:

  • Experiment with and internalize the scrum process
  • Create value by bringing together
    • Data visualization with D3
    • Webscraping
    • Consolidation and comparison of different sources
    • Text analysis
